## Findings / caveats

- **NVFP4 hurts reasoning more than retrieval.** GPQA −7.6 pts (0.581→0.505);
  RULER mean −2 pts. The quant cost concentrates in the thinking benchmark.
- **`ruler_vt` = 0.000 on BOTH checkpoints** (50/50, 0 errored, ~29 s). Identical
  zero across bf16 and nvfp4 ⇒ a systematic ruler_vt behaviour under non-thinking
  Qwen3-8B (answer format / variable-tracking), NOT a quant effect or endpoint
  failure. Excluded in the 12-task mean. Sample-check before trusting VT.
- **`niah_multikey_2` ~0.37 on both** is a task characteristic, not a quant delta.
- All jobs reached their intended totals (GPQA 198/198, each RULER task 50/50)
  with 0 errored trials, over cloudflared tunnels.
